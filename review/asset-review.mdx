The asset review page is where feedback and decisions stay attached to the asset being reviewed. Open any asset in a project and you land here - everything your team needs to evaluate a version, respond to comments, and move the work forward is in one place.

<Frame caption="Compare two versions side by side while reading the comment thread.">
  ![Asset review comparing two versions with the comments panel](/images/asset-review-compare-versions-comments.png)
</Frame>

## What you can do from the review page

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View the current version" icon="eye">
    See the latest uploaded version of the asset in the built-in viewer. Switch between previous versions from the version selector.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Leave and read comments" icon="message-circle">
    Add timestamped, annotated, or general comments directly on the asset. Read and reply to feedback from teammates and reviewers.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Assign to a teammate" icon="user-check">
    Assign the asset to a specific team member to signal who is responsible for acting on the current feedback.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Change the review status" icon="tag">
    Update the asset's status to reflect where it is in your workflow - for example, move it from **In Review** to **Changes Requested**.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Approve the current version" icon="circle-check">
    Mark the asset as approved when it meets the brief. The approval is recorded against the version that was open at the time.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Request revisions" icon="rotate-ccw">
    Flag that changes are needed. Pairing a revision request with timestamped or annotated comments gives the editor clear, actionable direction.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Share from the review page

You can generate an asset share link directly from the review page without leaving the asset. Select **Share** to open the link creation modal, configure your link settings, and send the link to an external reviewer. See [Create and Send Asset Share Links](/review/share-links) for the full steps and options.
