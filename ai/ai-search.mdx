Reloops includes a workspace-wide **Digital Asset Management (DAM) search** that lets you find any file across every project, folder, and version stack from a single screen. Instead of remembering where you saved a file, just describe what you're looking for in plain English.

## What you can search

The DAM search indexes every asset in your workspace, not just filenames. A single query can match against:

- **Title** - original filename and any renamed versions
- **Tags** - both manual tags and AI-generated tags (see [AI Tagging](/ai/ai-tagging))
- **Visual analysis** - what the AI actually sees inside the image or video
- **Captions / OCR** - text extracted from inside images, screenshots, and PDFs
- **Folder path** - where the asset lives in your project tree
- **URL** - source links attached to the asset
- **Metadata** - status, dates, format, version stacks

Each result card shows exactly which fields matched and the related terms that triggered the match, so you can see _why_ a file was returned.

## Running a search

1. Open your workspace and click **Search** in the left sidebar.
2. Type a natural-language query such as _"find me all the branding assets for reloops"_ or _"approved hero videos from last month"_.
3. Results stream in across all projects, ranked by relevance.
4. Use **Quick filters** or **Advanced** to narrow by format, status, project, date, or folder.

## Bulk actions on results

Once you have a result set you can:

- **Select all** matching assets with one click
- **Download matching assets** as a single bundle
- Open any asset directly into review without leaving the search surface

This makes it easy to pull together everything for a handoff, a client deliverable, or an archive without manually hunting through folders.

## AI credits

AI Search uses **AI credits** from your workspace balance. Credits are consumed when:

- A new query is run against the semantic index
- New or updated assets are re-indexed so they become searchable by meaning

Your remaining AI credit balance is visible in **Workspace settings -\> Billing & limits**. Stacking lifetime deal codes increases your monthly AI credit allowance along with seats and storage.

## Tips for better results

- Describe the _content_ of the asset, not just the filename ("dark background logo with gradient" works as well as `logo_v3_final.png`).
- Combine concepts: _"approved vertical videos for UGC campaign"_ will filter by status, orientation, and project context.
- Pair AI Search with [AI Tagging](/ai/ai-tagging) - the more your assets are auto-tagged, the more precise your search results become.
