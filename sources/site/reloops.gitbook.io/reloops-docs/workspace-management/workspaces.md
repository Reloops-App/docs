### 

Workspaces

Workspaces are the top-level homes for your projects, assets, team members and settings.

Use separate workspaces when work needs a clear boundary, such as a different client, brand, organization or production team.

App route: `/workspaces`

### 

What a workspace contains

- Projects and the assets inside them.
 
- Collections and reusable views.
 
- Team members and AI agents.
 
- Workspace settings, storage and billing limits.
 

Open the workspace that matches the client or team you are working with before uploading files or inviting reviewers.
