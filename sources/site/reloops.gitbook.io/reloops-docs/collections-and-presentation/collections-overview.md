Collections are presentation views over assets that already live in projects.

Use a collection when you need a clean gallery, approval set, client view, or reusable group of assets.

App routes: `/workspace/:workspaceId/collections` and `/workspace/:workspaceId/collections/:collectionId`

## 

What collections are good for

- Client review galleries.
 
- Final select sets.
 
- Cross-project asset groups.
 
- Reusable internal views.
 

Collections change how assets are presented. They do not move the original project files.
