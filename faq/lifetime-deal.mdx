## About Reloops

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Reloops and who is it for?">
    Reloops is a creative collaboration platform built for agencies, marketing teams, freelancers, and content creators who need to organize, review, and share creative work in one place. It replaces the messy mix of Google Drive links, Frame.io comments, WhatsApp screenshots, and back-and-forth emails with a single workspace for assets, feedback, approvals, and client sharing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Reloops different from Frame.io, Dropbox Replay, or Filestage?">
    Reloops combines asset management, version control, timecoded review, website review, kanban approval workflows, and branded client-facing collections in one tool. Most competitors only solve a single piece of the puzzle (video review, file sharing, or feedback). With Reloops you don't need to stitch multiple subscriptions together, and the lifetime deal means you pay once instead of monthly forever.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need any technical skills or onboarding to use Reloops?">
    No. If you can drag a file into a folder, you can use Reloops. Workspaces, projects, and collections are set up in a couple of clicks, and reviewers don't even need an account to leave timecoded comments or approve assets through a share link.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## The Lifetime Deal

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What exactly do I get with the lifetime deal?">
    You get lifetime access to your selected Reloops plan with a one-time payment - no recurring subscription, no surprise renewals. All future updates included in your tier are yours forever, and your storage, seats, and project limits are based on the tier (or stacked tiers) you purchase.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I stack codes to unlock more seats, storage, or projects?">
    Yes. You can stack multiple codes to increase your workspace limits (team members, storage, active projects, share links, etc.). The exact limits per stacked code are listed on the deal page on RocketHub.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is your refund policy?">
    Reloops comes with a 60-day money-back guarantee through RocketHub. If it's not the right fit for your workflow, request a refund within 60 days of purchase - no questions asked.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I keep getting updates and new features after I buy?">
    Yes. Lifetime deal customers receive all updates and new features that ship within their plan tier for as long as Reloops exists. You'll never be locked out of improvements you already paid for.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using Reloops day to day

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What kinds of files can I review in Reloops?">
    Reloops supports videos, images, PDFs, and live websites. You can leave timecoded comments on videos, pin comments on specific areas of images and PDFs, and drop notes directly on any live URL using website review mode.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my clients review and approve work without creating an account?">
    Yes. Generate a share link for any asset, project, or collection and your clients can preview, comment, and approve without signing up. You stay in control with expiry dates, passwords, and granular permissions on every link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Reloops support version control and approval workflows?">
    Yes. Every asset supports unlimited versions with side-by-side comparison, and projects come with a built-in kanban board (In Progress, In Review, Approved, etc.) so nothing slips through the cracks. Approvals are logged in the activity timeline for full accountability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I white-label collections for my clients?">
    Yes. Collections can be styled with your branding, custom layouts, and sorting so the final deliverable feels like a polished portfolio - not a generic file dump.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where is my data stored and is it secure?">
    Your files are stored on secure, encrypted cloud infrastructure. All transfers use TLS, and share links can be protected with passwords and expiry dates so only the right people see your work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my data if I cancel or stop using Reloops?">
    You can export your assets at any time. Since this is a lifetime deal, there's no cancellation - your workspace stays active for as long as Reloops is running.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
