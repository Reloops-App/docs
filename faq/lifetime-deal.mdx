<AccordionGroup>

Most competitors only solve a single piece of the puzzle (video review, file sharing, or feedback). With Reloops you don't need to stitch multiple subscriptions together, and the lifetime deal means you pay once instead of monthly forever.

## AI features & credits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are AI Search and AI Tagging included in the lifetime deal?">
    Yes. Your lifetime deal includes access to [AI Search](/ai/ai-search) and [AI Tagging / Smart Metadata](/ai/ai-tagging) so every asset you upload is automatically described, tagged, and made searchable in natural language across your workspace.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do AI credits work?">
    AI-powered actions (auto-tagging on upload, Smart Metadata regeneration, and semantic search queries) consume **AI credits** from your workspace balance. Your tier comes with a fixed monthly allowance of AI credits - the exact number is listed on the deal page on RocketHub.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do AI credits roll over or expire?">
    AI credits are **limited per month and expire at the end of each billing cycle** - they do not roll over. Each month your workspace is topped back up to your tier's allowance. If you run out before the month resets, uploads still succeed but new assets won't be auto-tagged and AI Search queries will be paused until the next refill.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I need more AI credits?">
    You can top up additional AI credits at any time from **Workspace settings -\> Billing & limits**. Top-up credits are consumed only after your monthly allowance is used up.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I search inside text on images, screenshots, and PDFs?">
    Yes. OCR runs on uploaded images and PDFs, so any text inside the file becomes searchable - find that one screenshot with the right headline in seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I create collections from search results?">
    Yes. Run any AI or filter search and save it as a **smart collection** that updates dynamically as new matching assets are uploaded - or as a **static collection** that locks in the current results. See [Collections](/collections/collections-overview).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Asset management & DAM

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I organize files into folders and nested libraries?">
    Yes. Every project supports unlimited nested folders, and you can upload an entire folder structure from your computer in one drag - subfolders are preserved automatically. See [Folders & Organization](/projects/folders-and-organization).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I move or copy assets between folders and projects without losing data?">
    Yes. **Move to** and **Copy to** actions let you reorganize across folders and even across projects in the same workspace. Comments, versions, status, share links, and AI tags travel with the asset.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I browse assets visually instead of by filename?">
    Yes. Every view (Files, Kanban, Collections) is thumbnail-first with status badges, so you find work by what it looks like, not by remembering filenames.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Reloops support versioning?">
    Yes. Stack unlimited versions on any asset, switch between them in one click, and use side-by-side **version comparison** to spot differences during review.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I track review progress visually?">
    Yes. Every project includes a **Kanban board** with status columns (In Progress, In Review, Approved, etc.) so the team always knows what needs attention. See [Kanban Board](/projects/kanban-status-workflow).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I bulk-tag or bulk-action multiple assets at once?">
    Yes. Multi-select assets to download, move, copy, share, or change status in bulk. AI auto-tagging happens automatically on upload, so most assets are tagged before you even open them.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## File formats & media review

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which file types can I review in Reloops?">
    Images (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, SVG), videos (MP4, MOV, WebM and more), PDFs, and live websites. Design files like PSD and AI have partial preview support. Audio review is supported with waveform comments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I review live websites and web prototypes?">
    Yes - this is a Reloops superpower most competitors don't have. Drop in any URL and leave pinned comments and notes directly on the live page. See [Website Review](/review/website-review).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I leave timecoded comments on videos?">
    Yes. Click anywhere on the timeline to attach a comment to that exact moment. Reviewers see the playhead jump to the right spot when they click a comment. See [Asset Review](/review/asset-review).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I see social media safe zones when reviewing video and image content?">
    Yes. Preview your assets with **safe zones** for Instagram Stories, Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and more - so nothing important gets cropped by platform UI. See [Leave Comments](/review/leave-comments).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I preview a video on phone, tablet, or desktop view ports?">
    Yes. Reloops can render videos inside common device frames (iPhone, etc.) so clients see exactly how the asset will appear in the wild.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I compare two versions side by side?">
    Yes. Open any asset, switch to **Compare Versions**, and review two revisions side by side with synchronized playback.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Reloops handle large media files?">
    Yes. Cloud-streamed playback means reviewers never download multi-GB files just to leave a comment - assets stream instantly from the browser.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

    EDL and Premiere XML comment export are **in active testing** and will roll out to lifetime deal users as part of free updates.

    Not yet. Slack notifications and Google Drive / Dropbox import are on the roadmap. You can still upload from any cloud drive manually today.

    Not in the current release. Reloops handles storage for you on encrypted cloud infrastructure included with your plan.
