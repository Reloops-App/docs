Reloops is a creative review and collaboration platform that gives teams one place to upload assets, gather feedback, track approval status, and share work with clients or stakeholders - without scattered emails or disconnected tools. Whether you are reviewing video, images, documents, or live websites, Reloops keeps every comment, version, and decision tied directly to the file it belongs to.

## Core building blocks

Reloops builds on four main concepts:

- **Workspaces** - A workspace is your top-level container. It holds all the projects, team members, and settings for a client, brand, or team. Every project and asset lives inside a workspace.
- **Projects** - A project groups the files and review progress for a body of work. Use projects to separate campaigns, deliverables, or client engagements.
- **Assets** - An asset is any individual file - a video, image, PDF, website capture, or other media - that you upload for review. Assets hold all comments, versions, and status history.
- **Collections** - A collection is a curated selection of assets presented as a visual gallery. Collections let you showcase or deliver a set of files without moving the source assets.
