Notifications let you stay on top of review activity without opening every project manually. Whenever a teammate leaves a comment, tags you in feedback, approves or rejects a review, or updates a file, Reloops logs that event and surfaces it in your notifications panel. Open the panel when you return from a break or context-switch between clients to quickly see what changed and what needs a response.

## What notifications track

The panel captures three categories of activity:

- **Comments and mentions** — feedback left on assets and any place a teammate has tagged you directly
- **Review activity** — approvals, rejections, and status changes on reviews you are involved in
- **File updates** — asset-level changes that may need your attention, such as new versions or replacements

## Notifications panel tabs

The panel groups all activity into focused tabs so you can scan only what matters to you right now.

| Tab | What it shows | When to use it |
|---|---|---|
| **All** | The complete, unfiltered activity feed across every category | Get a full picture of everything that has happened recently in your workspace |
| **Mentions** | Only the events where a teammate has tagged you by name | Catch direct feedback quickly without scrolling past unrelated activity |
| **Reviews** | Review actions and decisions — approvals, rejections, and status changes | Check the state of in-progress or completed reviews at a glance |
| **Files** | Asset-level updates such as new file versions or replacements | Identify files that have changed and may need re-review or sign-off |

## When to use the notifications panel

Open the panel any time you need to know what changed since you last checked in. Common moments include:

- **After a review deadline** — scan the Reviews tab to confirm all decisions are in
- **When you re-open Reloops** — start with All to get a snapshot of recent activity before diving into a project
- **After sharing assets for feedback** — check Mentions to see if anyone has tagged you with a question or concern
- **Before uploading a new version** — check Files to confirm no one is still reviewing the current version
